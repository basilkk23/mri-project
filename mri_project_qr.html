Open-Bore MRI Helmet

Portable ultra-low-field MRI design using optimized permanent magnet arrays

🧠 The Problem

Traditional MRI machines are expensive, bulky, and claustrophobic.

💡 Our Solution

We designed an open-bore helmet MRI to improve comfort while maintaining magnetic field performance.

⚙️ Key Results

• ~60 mT field strength
• ~40% improvement in homogeneity
• HPC optimization using ARC cluster
